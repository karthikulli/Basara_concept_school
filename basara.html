    Registration Process:

    Admission is granted to classes LKG to standard X. Application form and prospectus may be obtained from the school office.

    Documents Required:

    1. Student Aadhar Xerox
    2. Transfer certificate
    3. Study certificate of previous school
    4. 3 passport size photoes of student
    5. xerox of student progress Report

    The selection and admission will be made by the principal after scrutinizing the application and assessing the student's performance in the previous class.

    School Visit:

    You are most welcome to visit the school and have an informal discussion with or without your child. This can be done by fixing a prior appointment with the Concerned Person.

    Admissions during the year:

    Parents may register at any time during the year for admission in the current year and will be interviewed whenever a place is available.

    Admission:

    On being offered a place parents will be given two weeks time to pay the admission fee and security deposit to secure the place. Parents further need to fill and submit various forms and declarations that would be given along with admissions acceptance letter. Before allowing the student to take up his/her place a transfer certificate should be lodged with the admissions office from the earlier school of the student.

    ATAL THINKERING
    LAB

    Atal Tinkering Lab (ATL) - A Hub for Innovation and Learning:

    The Atal Tinkering Lab (ATL) at Basara is a state-of-the-art facility designed to nurture creativity, critical thinking, and hands-on learning among students. Supported by the Government of India's Atal Innovation Mission, ATL is part of a nationwide initiative to foster a culture of innovation and problem-solving skills in young minds.

    What is an Atal Tinkering Lab?

    An Atal Tinkering Lab is a dedicated innovation workspace where students can work with tools and equipment that encourage them to build projects across domains like science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics (STEAM). Through ATL, students have access to resources such as 3D printers, robotics kits, electronics, and other modern tools that bring their innovative ideas to life.

    Key Features and Offerings at ATL Basara

    • Hands-On Learning: With guidance from mentors, students get to work on real-world projects and apply theoretical knowledge through practical experiments.
    • Robotics & Electronics: Students explore fields like robotics and electronics, sparking interest in various tech-oriented fields and equipping them with future-ready skills.
    • 3D Printing & Prototyping: Access to 3D printing encourages students to visualize and create prototypes, allowing for a deeper understanding of design and innovation.
    • Coding & Software Development: The lab includes resources for learning programming and developing software, giving students a taste of coding and app development from an early age.

    Objectives of the ATL at Basara

    1. Cultivating Innovation: To foster creativity and inspire young learners to become thinkers, innovators, and creators.
    2. Developing Problem-Solving Skills: Encouraging students to approach real-life problems with innovative solutions.
    3. Promoting Collaboration: ATL provides a collaborative environment where students can work in teams, sharing ideas, and learning from each other.
    4. Building Future-Ready Skills: By engaging with technology early on, students develop critical skills that are increasingly important in today’s tech-driven world.

    Why ATL Matters for Our Students

    The ATL at Basara Concept School empowers students by providing a platform where they can experiment, fail, learn, and succeed. It encourages them to ask questions, think critically, and explore concepts outside of traditional textbooks. The lab aligns with our mission to provide a well-rounded education and helps prepare students for a rapidly evolving global landscape.

    Join the Innovation Journey!

    We invite our students to explore the endless possibilities at the Atal Tinkering Lab and become the thinkers and leaders of tomorrow. With ATL, Basara is proud to contribute to a future where innovation, creativity, and hands-on learning lead the way!

    Facilities

    Our Facilities at Basara Concept School

    Basara Concept School is renowned for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development. To ensure students have a comfortable and conducive living environment, the school offers exceptional hostel facilities designed to provide a home-like experience.

    Below are the key features of the hostel facilities:
    1. Accommodation
      • Separate hostel blocks for boys and girls, ensuring safety and privacy.
      • Spacious, well-ventilated dormitories with modern amenities.
      • Comfortable bunk beds or single beds with storage facilities.
      • Adequate lighting and proper ventilation in all rooms.
    2. Hygiene and Cleanliness
      • Regular cleaning and maintenance of hostel premises.
      • Availability of clean drinking water with water purifiers and coolers.
      • Attached or common washrooms with 24/7 hot water supply.
    3. Nutritious Meals
      • Well-balanced and nutritious meals served in the dining hall.
      • A menu curated by dieticians to cater to growing children’s needs.
      • Special meal arrangements during festivals and cultural events.
    4. Safety and Security
      • Round-the-clock security personnel stationed at hostel entrances
      • CCTV surveillance to monitor activities and ensure a safe environment.
      • Wardens and supervisors residing on campus to address emergencies.
    5. Health and Wellness
      • Regular medical check-ups and access to a first-aid center.
      • Tie-ups with local hospitals for emergency medical care.
      • Yoga and meditation sessions to promote mental well-being.

    The hostel at Basara Concept School ensures a nurturing environment where students can focus on their academics and personal growth while feeling at home. With its emphasis on safety, comfort, and overall development, it sets a benchmark for school hostels.

    Celebrations at Basara Concept School

    At Basara Concept School, we believe that celebrations play a vital role in enriching the school experience, fostering a sense of community, and encouraging the holistic development of our students. Our celebrations are designed to recognize important milestones, honor cultural diversity, promote creativity, and instill values of unity and teamwork among students, staff, and parents :

    Cultural and Religious Celebrations

    Our school embraces the diverse cultural and religious backgrounds of our students. Throughout the academic year, we celebrate various festivals such as Diwali, Eid, Christmas, and Pongal, along with national holidays like Republic Day and Independence Day.     Our Annual Day is one of the most anticipated events, where students showcase their talents through performances, speeches, dances, and drama. It is an exciting occasion that allows students to shine on stage and receive recognition for their hard work.
